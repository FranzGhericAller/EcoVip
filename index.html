About Us

History

Our Ecotourism Hub was created with support from the Erasmus+ project ECOViP. We have been dedicated to promoting sustainable tourism practices and supporting entrepreneurial activities within the tourism industry.

Vision and Mission

Our mission is to foster sustainable tourism practices, support local communities, and promote environmental conservation. We envision a world where tourism contributes positively to the environment and society.
